About Brandon Sung

My passion lies in an variety of works in art, clothing, furniture, businesses and code. I design graphic art on photoshop and illustrator, laser cut and etch photos, make wood furniture at the wood shop and replicate movie props. I am also working on designing/sewing my own clothing line. I create detailed embroidery files and sew them into custom clothes. In addition, I collect expensive shoes and clothing to resell. I worked on many businesses including a clothing heat sublimation printing company, fashion accessory brand, ethical fashion brand and a suscription based DIY toys. I also code on my free time. I am working on an app that webscrapes data about calories from Trim Dining hall menu. I play tennis and basketball for fun. I run cross country, work out, and played clarinet in my high school marching band. I also volunteer at Cradles to Crayons, Home for the Little Wanderers, Harvard Square Meals, STEAM After School Program at Jeffersons.
